Lasagna Recipe

The recipe stated is a quick and easy yet delicious lasagna recipe for those late evenings when the children are hungry and you are tired.

Only a few minutes of Preperation and then left to be baked in the oven. Quick and easy 30min for even the unexperienced.

Ingredients

  • 9 Lasagna noodles sheets
  • 1 tsp olive oil (pref. extra virgin)
  • 500g Ground beef
  • 1 Onoin
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 3 1/2 cups of cheese
  • 1 egg large
  • 1/4 cup Parmasen cheese
  • 2 tbsp parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C and lighly oil pot
  2. Cook lasagna sheets in pot of boiling salt water
  3. Heat olive oil in pan over medium heat. Add beef, onoin and cook until beef is brown. Season with salt,pepper and drain fat.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and Italian seasoning until well mixed
  5. In seperate bowl combine 1/2 cup cheese and egg to be set aside
  6. Spread 1 cup tomatoes at bottom of baking dish, top with 3 lasagna sheets, add cheese mixture. Repeat for another layer
  7. Top with chese, sheets and Parmasen
  8. Bake in oven for 35-45min or until bubbling
  9. Let it cool and serve garnished with parsley
